About Me

Hello, My name is Tarek Hamid. I'm passionate about biomedical signal processing, machine learning, wearables, and digital/mobile health.

I believe in a proactive approach to healthcare; using biomedical instrumentation and computer systems, we can help lead healthier lives, diagnose conditions earlier, and stop disease before it progresses. To create such devices requires highly interdisciplinary and collaborative teams, an environment I believe I thrive in.

I am currently a software engineer at JPMorgan Chase. Previously, I worked at the Department of Defense as an electrical engineer and a scientist at Johnson & Johnson. I believe my interdisciplinary background allows me to be a good and unique asset to the wearables and mHealth field.

In my spare time I enjoy playing basketball, volleyball, and overall staying active.
